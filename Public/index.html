About this Project:

Hi there! My name is Julian Ng. Welcome to our Movie Night and Game Night website, where my friends and I select movies to watch and games to play. We used to use Discord chats and third party polling sites to nominate and vote for our activities, which frequently got cluttered and disorganized. Futhermore, in order to keep track of the movies we had watched and the previous nominations, one of us kept a spreadsheet and manually updated it. I built this website to centralize our process all in one place, but after some thought and discussion with the group, I decided to also use this platform to share our history and how our procedure has evolved over the years.

To provide a little background on us, most of us met in our freshman year studying computer science at Purdue University. A few of us ended up becoming roommates for the following 3 years but for the most part we all took the same classes and studied together. Outside of the classroom we spent a lot of time playing games and watching movies and shows together. Towards the end of our senior year, we had a lot of activities planned in the downtime after final exams and leading up to graduation. Unfortunately, our graduation date was in May of 2020. Right in the middle of COVID-19 lockdowns, we were mailed our diplomas and had to find a way to celebrate at home. Either trying to start our careers or get into grad school, we struggled to stay connected from all over the country other than just hopping in a call and saying hi a couple times a week. That's when we came up with Game Night, and subsequently Movie Night. It was the perfect way to sync up a block of time out of our busy schedules to really have some fun like we used to back in college.

Today we continue to hold these Movie and Game nights as much as we can. When we first started we designated our Movie nights for Saturday night and Game nights on Tuesday night, and still keep to that today. Although we haven't been able to hold these events as consistenly as we used to back during lockdown, we have been able to see each other in person more often through vacations and travel, as most of the photos you can see above have been from the past few years. We will try to keep this website up to date as possible, so if you know us or are interested in our story stay connected with us!
